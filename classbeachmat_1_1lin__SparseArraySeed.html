template<class V, typename TIT>
class beachmat::lin_SparseArraySeed< V, TIT >

Sparse integer, logical or numeric matrices in the SparseArraySeed format from the DelayedArray package.

Template Parameters
VThe class of the Rcpp::Vector holding the R-level data for non-zero values.
